How To Import Custom Songs

Written Directions (From Video)
  • Step 1 – Download your custom song: find your song on ParaDB and download it to your device
  • Step 2 – Open AnExplorer: launch the AnExplorer app and navigate to the Archives section to locate your downloaded song ZIP
  • Step 3 – Copy the ZIP file: hold RT on the file and select Copy
  • Step 4 – Navigate to your Songs folder: go to Internal Storage → Paradiddle → Songs and paste the ZIP file here
  • Step 5 – Uncompress the ZIP: scroll to the bottom to find your pasted ZIP, hold RT, tap the 3 dots, and select Uncompress
  • Step 6 – Select all song contents: open the song folder and its subfolder, hold RT on any item, tap the 3 dots, select Select All, then choose Copy
  • Step 7 – Paste and clean up: go back out of the subfolder and paste the copied items, then delete the empty subfolder
  • 🎵 Your song is ready to play: launch the game and enjoy your custom track
Tips / Notes

📌 Make sure the custom song file is in the correct format before importing

📌 Follow the exact folder structure required by your game so it loads properly

📌 Make sure all subfolders are removed or the song will not load in game properly
